My Agile Journey

Kaya, with 22 years of experience in law enforcement, followed by two years in research, I have been embodying Agile principles long before my formal Scrum Master certifications. My passion lies in fostering collaboration, removing blockers, and servant leadership to create high-performing teams and leverage technology for impactful outcomes. I facilitated cross-functional discussions, prioritised tasks, and ensured alignment with stakeholders to deliver value-driven solutions.

I thrive in collaborative multi-agency work environments and I am eager to contribute my skills and expertise to a dynamic and inclusive team, where I can apply my Agile mindset to drive innovation and deliver exceptional results.
